ABOUT US

Started in 2021 by Shobhit Agarwal, Skyspace quickly became a well known firm, collaborating with various government agencies to plan and design apartment complexes and various buildings.
With over 3 years of experience, our architectural firm specializes in creating inspiring residential and commercial spaces. Having successfully completed 15 diverse projects, we pride ourselves on delivering innovative design solutions that blend functionality and aesthetics.
Our team is dedicated to transforming your vision into reality, whether you're building a cozy home or a dynamic commercial space. From concept to completion, we ensure every project is executed with precision and creativity. Let us help you design spaces that stand the test of time.
